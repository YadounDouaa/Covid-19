What Is COVID-19 ?

Covid-19 is :

Covid-19 (Coronavirus Disease-19), a respiratory disease caused by an emerging coronavirus, SARS-CoV-2. The epidemic started in the city of Wuhan, China in late December 2019 and has spread rapidly around the world.

What is syptoms of Covid-19 ?

The Syptoms is :

  • Cough
  • Fever
  • Breathing difficulties
  • Pneumonia in both lungs

There is a solution to fight against this virus ?

No! But ther is precautions for this virus

The Precautions is :

-Hands should be washed regularly and sterile gloves should be worn.

-Avoid (greeting / hug) people.

-Sneeze into your elbow or use a handkerchief.

-Wear a protective anti-bacterial mask and change it when you feel it is wet.

-Drink plenty of water.

-Avoid gatherings.

-Do not go outside unless it is an emergency.
